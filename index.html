About Terraria

Terraria is an action-adventure sandbox game. Explore, build, and fight your way through procedurally generated worlds.

In Terraria, the player explores a vast 2D world, mining resources, fighting enemies, and constructing buildings to protect against invasions.

With numerous biomes, bosses, and enemies, the game offers endless exploration and creativity.

Features include dynamic environments, day/night cycles, NPCs, and unique crafting systems.
